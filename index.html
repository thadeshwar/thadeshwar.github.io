I'm Abhi Thadeshwar, a graduate student in the department of Computer Science at University of Southern California. I grew up biking along the riverfront in a prominent town in India. My day usually varies between developing software and living vicariously through them.

I am currently working on computational models for framing theory at Information Sciences Institute under the supervision of Professor Andres Abeliuk and Professor Kristina Lerman.

Previously, I was an intern at the Indian Space Research Organization, where I helped implement atmospheric delay correction models to improve navigation. Before coming to the States, I did my undergraduation from Indus University with a focus on Computer Engineering.
